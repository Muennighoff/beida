## 4.排序

​	Merge()

> ```python
> A=[2,5,9,12,16,18]
> B=[1,3,7,11,13,15,17,19,20,22]
> def merge(L,R):
>     i=0
>     j=0
>     ret=[]
>     while i<len(L) and j<len(R):
>         if L[i]<R[j]:
>             ret.append(L[i])
>             i+=1
>         else:
>             ret.append(R[j])
>             j+=1
>     while i<len(L):
>         ret.append(L[i])
>         i+=1
>     while j<len(R):
>         ret.append(R[j])
>         j+=1
>     return ret
> print(merge(A,B))
> ```
